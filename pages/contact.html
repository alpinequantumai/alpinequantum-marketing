Common questions

What to expect after you reach out.

How quickly will I hear back?

Within one business day. Initial replies come from a senior engineer or engagement lead — not a sales rep — with honest first thoughts on scope, feasibility, and timeline.

Do I need to have everything figured out first?

No. Most of our best engagements start with a rough idea and a real business problem. We help shape scope during discovery. If your initiative is too early to scope, we'll say so.

What does a typical engagement look like?

Most projects run 8–16 weeks for an initial phase. We work in agile sprints with weekly demos, full visibility via your tools (Jira, GitHub, Slack), and a senior architect leading from start to finish.

How is pricing structured?

Fixed-price by default, with milestone-based payments tied to accepted deliverables. We also offer time & materials and retainer models when they fit better. Every engagement is governed by our standard MSA.

Who owns the IP?

By default, you do. Every line of code, every model, every piece of documentation. We retain ownership only of underlying tools and frameworks that pre-exist the engagement.

Do you sign NDAs before discovery calls?

Yes, gladly. We have a mutual NDA we can send, or we can use yours. Just mention it in your inquiry.
