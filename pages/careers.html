Careers

Join engineers who actually ship.

We're building a small team of senior engineers, architects, and operators who want to do the best work of their careers — on production systems that matter to real businesses.

Why us

Five reasons to join.

We're deliberately a small, senior team. The bar for joining is high — but so is what you'll work on, who you'll work with, and how much your work will compound on itself.

If you're a junior engineer looking for mentorship, this isn't the right place. If you're a senior engineer tired of slide-deck consulting and want to ship real systems again, read on.

01

Hands-on work, all the way up

Even our most senior engineers stay in code. No "architecture astronauts," no PowerPoint principals. If you stop writing code, you stop being effective at what we do.

02

Small teams, big problems

Most engagements are run by 3-5 engineers. You'll own real scope, make real decisions, and see real outcomes. No 50-person pyramid where you contribute to a single slide.

03

Senior colleagues you'll learn from

Everyone you'll work with has shipped production systems at scale. The level of conversation, design review, and code review reflects that. You'll grow faster than at most places.

04

Compensation that respects expertise

We pay at the top of the Indian market for senior engineers. We don't do equity (you've already worked at startups), but we do annual performance bonuses tied to project outcomes.

05

Work that gets harder over time

The intentional design of our portfolio is that each engagement teaches the team something we didn't know before. Boredom is the failure mode we work hardest to prevent.

The process

What to expect.

A short, respectful process — typically 2-3 weeks from first conversation to offer. We move fast for strong candidates.

step 01

Intro call

30 min with a senior engineer. We learn about your work; you learn about ours. No technical screening — just a real conversation about whether this is interesting to both sides.

step 02

Technical conversation

90 min walking through a real problem from our work. No whiteboarding algorithms, no LeetCode. We're looking for how you think — how you'd approach an actual system.

step 03

Take-home (optional)

A short engineering exercise you can do in 3-4 hours. Optional — we'll skip this if your existing work history makes it unnecessary. You'll be paid for your time.

step 04

Founder conversation

A final conversation with one of the founders. We talk about what you're looking for next, what we can offer, and decide if there's a match.

Don't see your role?

We're growing thoughtfully. If you're a senior engineer, architect, or operator who'd be a fit for what we do, we'd still like to hear from you — even if there's no current opening.
