What we solve

Common challenges.

Common challenges across retail clients. Most engagements address two or three of these.

01

Unified commerce & omnichannel

A single view of the customer across web, mobile, physical store, marketplace, and service touchpoints — and the architecture that makes it real, not slideware.

02

Personalization at scale

Real-time recommendation, search ranking, content personalization, and pricing intelligence — operating on hundreds of millions of customer interactions.

03

Inventory & supply chain visibility

Real-time inventory truth across stores, DCs, and 3PLs — with the ML-driven forecasting and replenishment systems that operate on it.

04

Customer data platforms (CDP)

A unified customer profile across all systems — identity resolution, event collection, segmentation, activation — usable by marketing, product, and service teams.

05

Marketing automation & analytics

Marketing measurement that survives iOS 14+ and cookie deprecation. Attribution, MMM, and customer lifetime value modeling that informs real budget decisions.

06

In-store technology & associate enablement

Mobile POS, clienteling, in-store apps, and the technology that empowers associates to deliver service that competes with digital-native rivals.
